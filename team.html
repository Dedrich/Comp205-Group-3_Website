Founder and CEO
Elon Musk

Elon Musk is the founder, CEO, and lead designer at SpaceX. He is also the co-founder, CEO and product architect of Tesla as well as the co-founder and chairman of OpenAI. In the past he co-founded and sold PayPal and Zip2.

President, COO and Board of Directors
Gwynne Shotwell

As President and COO of SpaceX, Gwynne Shotwell is responsible for day-to-day operations and for managing all customer and strategic relations to support company growth. She joined SpaceX in 2002 as Vice President of Business Development and built the Falcon vehicle family manifest to nearly 50 launches, representing nearly $5 billion in revenue. Shotwell is a member of the SpaceX Board of Directors.

Vice President of Quality
James Henderson

As Vice President of Quality Assurance, James leads SpaceX in setting new industry standards for reliability and safety in space technology. James comes to us from BMW, where he achieved outstanding success in quality management, production, product launch, and lean manufacturing.

Senior Vice President and General Counsel
Tim Hughes

As Senior Vice President and General Counsel, Mr. Hughes leads the company's legal, regulatory, and government affairs efforts. Joining SpaceX in 2005 as its first in-house counsel, Tim has defined the legal and government affairs functions from the ground up. Based in SpaceX's Washington, DC office, his responsibilities span the company's corporate, contracting, export control, insurance, litigation and launch licensing portfolios, as well as SpaceX's federal and state government affairs agenda.
